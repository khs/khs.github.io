Oil Price Outlook

Market-implied crude oil prices with estimated uncertainty ranges. Pump price estimates include refining margins, taxes, and regional variation. Data updated weekly.

History shown: 6 months

Dark band: ±1σ (68% interval)  |  Light band: ±2σ (95% interval). Bands use 33% annualised vol, symmetric up/down. Uncertainty plateaus past ~2.5 years due to long-run mean reversion. Calibrated from 20-year WTI backtest (EIA RCLC1–4 vs EIA spot).
